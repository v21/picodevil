hello!

picodevil is here to help you livecode visuals with videoclips! it's free to use and open source. it runs in your browser - yes, this browser here!

the best way to get started is to click on the examples tab above and have a poke through those. you can also look at the guide %% make and link and the reference %% link.

once you've played with it for a while, you will probably want to use your own videclips. if they're hosted somewhere that supports hotlinking, you can paste the URL into the videos tab. if not, you will want to host them yourself - this part is trickier, especially as this tool works best if the videos have been transcoded especially for it. see this guide %%% for more details.

about

picodevil is built on top of Strudel, a livecoding language where you make music out of patterns. i took the pattern bit and changed it to be about videoclips instead of audioclips.

picodevil is open source - you can redistribute and/or modify it under the terms of the GNU Affero General Public License.
